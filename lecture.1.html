Website development

What is website?

The definition of a website is a page or collection of pages on the World Wide Web that contains
specific information which was all provided by one person or entity and traces back to a common
Uniform Resource Locator (URL). Facebook and MySpace are examples of websites.

How does website works?

At its simplest, a website provides a quick and easy way of communicating information between buyers and sellers
You can list your opening hours, contact information show images of your location or product,
and use contact forms to facilitate enquiries from potential customers or feedback from
existing ones.

What is coding and how is it used?

Coding creates a set of instructions for computers to follow
. These instructions determine what actions a computer can and cannot take.
Coding allows programmers to build programs, such as websites and apps.
Computer programmers can also tell computers how to process data in better, faster ways.
